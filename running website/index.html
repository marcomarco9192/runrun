Run in Venice!

Join the tour to explore Venice's main attractions and promenades. During your run with us, you'll have the opportunity to savor the tranquil morning views of the city, immerse yourself in its vibrant rhythm, and even feel like a Venetian on a morning jog. We'll also capture numerous photos to help you cherish the beauty of Venice!

Venice Sunrise Running Tour

About your guide

Hey there :) My name is Denis. I have been a resident of Venice for over 10 years, and as an avid runner, I am thrilled to offer you an unforgettable experience of a serene morning city center, along with showcasing the main water-based attractions of the city. An added bonus for you is the opportunity to start your day with a refreshing workout and good vibes, which will surely accompany you throughout the rest of the day. Ready?

KNOW BEFORE YOU GO

Runners of various abilities and paces are welcome! This isn't a competitive race but rather an enjoyable journey through Venice at a relaxed pace. Our approach is inclusive, with the group adjusting to the pace of the slowest runner, ensuring no one gets left behind. Rest assured, you're in good company!

Regardless, it's important to be an active individual who takes pleasure in physical activity and can comfortably run a minimum of 5 kilometers (or 3 miles) without interruption before considering participation in this tour.

We only allow minors of 18 years old when they have the expressed authorization of their parents or responsible adults

Our experience starts on time. To avoid ruining the other guests' experience, we kindly ask you to arrive at our meeting point around 5 minutes earlier.

Our tours take place regardless of the weather conditions. If the weather becomes unfavorable, we will get in touch with you to discuss cancellations and rescheduling. If you don't receive a message from us, it indicates that the tour is proceeding as planned.

We want to ensure that your Venice Running Morning Tour is enjoyable and safe. However, it's important to note that we do not assume responsibility for any unforeseen circumstances or events during the tour. While we make every effort to provide a positive experience, participants should be aware of their own limitations and act responsibly. Safety is our top priority, and we encourage all participants to follow our guidelines and recommendations to have a memorable and secure running adventure in Venice.
